# Proof-to-Product Promotion Law

Last updated: 2026-04-13 17:45 EDT
Status: ACTIVE

## Rule
A product candidate must not be sold as mature until it has:
1. a clear expensive problem
2. a repeatable proof artifact
3. bounded delivery scope
4. a packaging surface
5. a promotion decision recorded in canonical doctrine

## Promotion classes
- internal only
- pilot-ready
- sellable high-ticket
- sellable recurring
- compounding asset
