# Nonstop Closure Doctrine

Last updated: 2026-04-13 16:50 EDT
Status: ACTIVE

## Rule
Once a governed internal closure sequence begins, do not stop at intermediate milestones when the next closure step is clear, internal, low-risk, and verifiable.

## Stop conditions
Stop only when:
- the task is actually finished
- the next move crosses a destructive or external boundary
- the control path would be endangered
- ambiguity would make the action reckless

## Reporting rule
Report meaningful terminal state, not conversational pause points.
