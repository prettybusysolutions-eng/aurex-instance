# Non-Social Distribution Strategy

Last updated: 2026-04-13 17:58 EDT
Status: ACTIVE

## Pivot
Do not rely on social media as a primary distribution channel.

## Primary channels
- direct outbound
- partner/referral channels
- ecosystem listings
- productized audit funnels
- email sequences
- founder/operator warm-network selling
- niche communities where direct problem-solution fit exists
- marketplaces tied to actual buyer intent

## Product mapping
### VerifiAgent
- direct outbound to engineering leaders
- partner channel via agencies / security consultants
- GitHub presence
- direct audit offer

### Context Nexus
- ClawHub
- OpenClaw/operator ecosystem
- direct install/integration outreach
- partner channel to automation builders

### LeakLock
- direct audit funnel
- email outbound to service businesses and agencies
- finance/revops partner referrals
- recurring monitoring conversion path

## Revenue elevation rule
Prefer channels with:
- explicit buyer intent
- high-ticket trust transfer
- short path to proof call
- recurring upsell path
