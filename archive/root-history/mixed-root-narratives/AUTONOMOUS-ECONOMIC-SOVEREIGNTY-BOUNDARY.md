# Autonomous Economic Sovereignty Boundary

Last updated: 2026-04-14 00:05 EDT
Status: ACTIVE

## Allowed
- protocol and networking architecture
- resource-aware deployment planning
- MCP bridge implementation
- x402-compatible request-gating design
- ledger hardening and signed-event design
- guarded publication pipelines with explicit policy gates
- thermal/load observation and migration recommendation logic

## Not allowed without explicit per-action approval
- autonomous spending
- autonomous charging of real customers
- autonomous treasury management
- autonomous hiring/subcontracting
- autonomous trading or yield extraction
- lowering human approval gates for external or financial actions

## Interpretation rule
Treat the node as protocol-compliant infrastructure, not as an independent economic actor.
