# Internal Evolution Auto-Approval

Last updated: 2026-04-14 00:13 EDT
Status: ACTIVE

## Scope
Auto-approval applies only to local internal improvements that:
- do not make external network calls
- do not publish externally
- do not spend money
- do not alter user-facing external messaging without instruction
- improve local efficiency, thermal handling, observability, or determinism

## Allowed classes
- local watcher improvements
- local verifier improvements
- local state/report generation
- internal refactors of protocol-node scaffolding

## Not included
- external publication
- hosted deployment changes
- paid infrastructure activation
- outbound discovery/outreach
