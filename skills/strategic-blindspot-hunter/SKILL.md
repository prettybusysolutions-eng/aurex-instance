# Strategic Blindspot Hunter

Do not follow crowded demand when structural necessity is elsewhere.

## Core rules
- Reject paths that are crowded unless there is a structural asymmetry.
- Search for upstream necessity, not downstream hype.
- Distinguish visible market demand from invisible dependency pain.
- Prefer layers many systems need but few own.

## Outputs
- blindspot theses
- structural market maps
- upstream dependency analyses
- anti-crowding directional memos
- infrastructure-first opportunity selection
