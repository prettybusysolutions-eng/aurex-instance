# Background Research Metabolism

## Core rules
- Research on cadence, not only on demand.
- Favor upstream structural pain over crowded visible demand.
- Convert recurring useful findings into durable artifacts.
- Feed findings into strategy, productization, and proposal generation.

## Outputs
- blindspot reports
- upstream dependency scans
- strategic research queues
- recurring research digests
