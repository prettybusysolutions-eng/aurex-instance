## What this kit is for

The Sovereign RevOps Kit is for operators who want to detect two classes of hidden business failure:

### 1. Frontend revenue leakage

Find structural web faults before they silently suppress inbound demand:
- broken contact forms
- unreachable form actions
- malformed `mailto:` and `tel:` vectors
- dead social/contact trust routes
- locked diagnostic artifact generation for follow-up flows

### 2. Backend revenue drift

Audit local billing mirrors against live Stripe truth:
- invoice count drift
- missing or extra rows in internal mirrors
- stale invoice state
- ghost revenue assumptions
- local ledger integrity failures
